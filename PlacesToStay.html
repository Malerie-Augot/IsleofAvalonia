Places To Stay


The Eluna Fairy Tree-Top Hotel

The hotel offers a variety of luxurious accommodations, each meticulously crafted with attention to detail and infused with fairy magic. Guests can choose from spacious treehouses suspended among the treetops or elegant suites nestled within the trunks of towering trees. The interiors are adorned with intricate woodwork, delicate fairy lights, and soft, natural fabrics that create a cozy and inviting atmosphere


The hotel's spa offers rejuvenating treatments inspired by nature and magic. Guests can indulge in fairy-infused massages, mystical facials using herbal potions, or unwind in natural hot springs infused with healing properties. The spa's open-air design provides breathtaking views of the forest, allowing guests to connect with the surrounding beauty while enjoying their treatments.


At the Eluna Fairy Tree Top Hotel, every guest is treated to a range of magical amenities that reflect the enchanting nature of Avaloria. Each accommodation is equipped with an enchantment crystal, allowing guests to adjust the lighting and ambiance to their liking. Delicate wind chimes and soothing forest sounds fill the air, creating a serene and relaxing environment.


The hotel offers an exceptional dining experience at the Eluna Fairy Cafe, that celebrates the flavors of Avalonia. The restaurant, nestled among the treetops, provides panoramic views of the forest and offers a blend of traditional and contemporary cuisine prepared by skilled fairy chefs.


The Eluna Fairy Tree Top Hotel in Avalonia offers an extraordinary escape into a world of enchantment and tranquility. With its harmonious integration with nature, luxurious accommodations, magical amenities, and a range of captivating activities, it creates an unforgettable experience that allows guests to truly embrace the mystical wonders of Avaloria.

Pirate Ship Seascape

For those seeking a truly unique and adventurous stay on the mystical island of Avalonia, the Pirate Ship Accommodations offer an extraordinary experience reminiscent of a bygone era of swashbuckling pirates and high-seas adventures. These ship-inspired accommodations provide a blend of nostalgia, coupled with modern comfort, and the allure of the open seas.


These ships are meticulously crafted to resemble authentic pirate ships of old, adorned with weathered wooden planks, ropes, and tall masts. The ships are anchored on the shore, creating a sense of permanence while evoking the thrill of life on the high seas. The interiors are thoughtfully designed to reflect the ambiance of a pirate vessel, featuring rich wooden furnishings, maritime-themed decor, and details that transport guests into a world of pirates and buried treasures.


Guests have the opportunity to choose from a variety of accommodations aboard ship. The Captain's Quarters, the most luxurious option, offers a spacious and opulent retreat. With a comfortable king-sized bed, a private sitting area, and panoramic views of Avalonia's coastline. The ship also features cozy cabins, each designed to provide a sense of adventure and comfort. Ranges of bed sizes to accommodate individuals, couples, or families, along with nautical-inspired decor that adds to the immersive experience. The portholes in the cabins offer glimpses of the shimmering ocean, allowing guests to feel as if they are sailing the open seas.


During your stay, guests have access to a range of amenities that combine modern comforts with a touch of maritime charm. Common areas include a spacious deck where guests can relax in wooden deck chairs, soak in the sun, or enjoy the breathtaking views of the island and its surrounding waters. The ship's galley, converted into a dining area, serves delicious meals inspired by pirate lore and traditional island cuisine. Guests can savor the flavors of freshly caught seafood, hearty stews, and tropical fruits, all while immersed in the seafaring atmosphere of the pirate ship.

Serenity Cavern Oceanview Spa Retreat

The accommodations at Serenity Caverns Oceanview Spa Retreat are designed to blend seamlessly with the natural surroundings. Guests have the opportunity to stay in spacious and beautifully appointed cave-like suites, carved into the mountainside. These suites feature earthy tones, natural textures, and soft lighting that create a cozy and intimate atmosphere. From their private terrace, guests can take in panoramic ocean views that stretch as far as the eye can see, immersing themselves in the beauty and tranquility of Avalonia's coastal landscapes.


Each suite boasts a private terrace that offers uninterrupted vistas of the azure ocean. Guests can relax on their terrace, surrounded by lush greenery and cascading waterfalls, and marvel at the vastness of the sea. The rhythmic sound of crashing waves and the salty breeze create a sense of serenity and connection to the ocean's natural rhythm.


Guests are treated to an array of spa and wellness treatments inspired by the island's natural elements. The spa is nestled within the heart of the mountain, featuring soothing caves adorned with warm lighting and natural stone formations. Highly skilled therapists provide personalized treatments using organic, locally sourced ingredients, such as healing mineral-rich muds,and aromatic herbs, and floral essences. As guests indulge in rejuvenating therapies, they can enjoy glimpses of the sparkling ocean through carefully placed windows, allowing the sight and sound of the sea to enhance the healing experience.


A highlight of the Serenity Caverns Oceanview Spa Retreat is the underground thermal pools and waterfall showers. These natural wonders are tucked away within the mountain, creating an intimate and secluded space for relaxation and rejuvenation. Guests can immerse themselves in the warm healing waters of the thermal pools, letting the soothing minerals wash away any tension. As they stand under the invigorating waterfall showers, they can bask in the panoramic ocean views, feeling refreshed and revitalized by the union of water and nature.
