Unicorn-back Riding

On the magical island of Avalonia, visitors have the extraordinary opportunity to embark on an enchanting unicorn-back riding experience. This unforgettable adventure allows guests to immerse themselves in the mystical world of unicorns, forging a connection with these majestic creatures while exploring the island's breathtaking landscapes.


Upon arrival at the designated unicorn stables, guests are warmly greeted by a herd of graceful and ethereal unicorns. These magnificent creatures are adorned with gleaming coats in various shades, from pure white to shimmering pastels. Guests have the opportunity to approach the unicorns with gentle respect and form a connection, fostering a sense of trust and companionship before the riding experience begins.


The tour is led by experienced guides who possess a deep understanding of these mythical creatures. The guides ensure the safety and well-being of both guests and unicorns throughout the journey. Each guest is carefully paired with a unicorn based on compatibility and comfort, allowing for a personalized and intimate riding experience.


As guests ride alongside their unicorn companions, a profound sense of connection and unity is fostered. The gentle and intuitive nature of the unicorns allows riders to feel a deep bond with these mythical beings, creating a shared experience of trust, harmony, and adventure. The ride becomes a symbiotic journey, where both rider and unicorn embark on an unforgettable exploration of Avalonia's magical landscapes.


The unicorn-back riding experience in Avalonia is a testament to the island's mystical allure. By venturing into the world of unicorns, guests are transported to a realm of wonder and beauty, forging a connection with these mythical creatures, and creating memories that will last a lifetime. It is an adventure that invites guests to embrace their sense of imagination and embark on an unforgettable journey through the magical landscapes of Avaloria.

Swimming with Mermaids

One of the most enchanting and sought-after experiences in Avalonia is the opportunity to swim alongside the graceful and mesmerizing mermaids. This guest outing allows visitors to immerse themselves in the mystical underwater world, forging a connection with these legendary beings and experiencing the magic of the ocean firsthand.


The swimming excursion takes place in a hidden underwater oasis, a place of serene beauty teeming with colorful coral reefs, vibrant marine life, and sunken treasures. Crystal-clear waters provide visibility that allows guests to fully appreciate the mesmerizing sights that await beneath the surface.


During the swim, guests have the opportunity to interact with the mermaids, who gracefully glide through the water, performing elegant acrobatics and playful tricks. The mermaids communicate through a combination of fluid movements, gestures, and a melodic language understood by those attuned to the magic of Avaloria. Guests can engage in playful games, swim alongside the mermaids, or simply observe their serene beauty.


The underwater oasis offers guests an array of magical moments. Schools of iridescent fish dart playfully between coral formations, while sea turtles glide gracefully through the water. The mermaids share their deep knowledge of the ocean, pointing out hidden wonders, coral gardens, and even the occasional sunken ship or buried treasure, adding an element of excitement and adventure to the experience.


Swimming with the mermaids in Avalonia is an otherworldly experience that allows visitors to transcend the boundaries of the human realm and venture into a realm of enchantment. It is an opportunity to embrace the mysteries of the ocean, connect with the mermaids, and forge a deep appreciation for the intricate beauty that lies beneath Avalonia's breathtaking waters.

Crocodile Lagoon

For those seeking a thrilling and up-close encounter with Avalonia's formidable yet fascinating creatures, the Crocodile Lagoon Guest Tour offers a unique opportunity to observe and learn about the island's resident crocodiles in their natural habitat. Led by experienced guides, this tour provides a safe and informative experience that allows guests to witness the power and beauty of these ancient reptiles.


The Crocodile Lagoon Guest Tour includes strategically placed observation points and platforms that allow guests to view the crocodiles from various angles and vantage points. These platforms are designed to provide clear visibility while ensuring the safety of the guests, enabling them to observe the crocodiles' movements, basking behaviors, and potential interactions within their social hierarchy.


Throughout the tour, guides share educational insights about the crocodiles' natural history, biology, and ecological importance within Avalonia's delicate ecosystem. Guests learn about the crocodile's feeding habits, reproductive cycles, and their crucial role in maintaining the balance of the lagoon's ecosystem. The guides also dispel myths and misconceptions surrounding crocodiles, fostering a deeper understanding and appreciation for these ancient creatures.


Guests are encouraged to bring cameras or smartphones to capture the mesmerizing moments of their crocodile encounters. Professional photographers may also be available to offer expertly taken photos as mementos of this extraordinary experience. Additionally, souvenir shops nearby may provide a selection of crocodile-themed merchandise, allowing guests to take home a tangible reminder of their visit.


The Crocodile Lagoon Guest Tour in Avalonia offers an exhilarating and educational adventure, allowing guests to witness the power and majesty of these ancient reptiles while gaining a greater understanding of their role in the island's intricate ecosystem. It is an experience that encourages both respect for nature and the preservation of Avalonia's natural wonders.

Eluna Fairy Cafe

Nestled amidst the enchanting landscape of Avalonia, the Eluna Fairy Cafe offers a truly magical dining experience that delights the senses and transports guests into a world of whimsy and culinary excellence. From the moment guests step foot into the cafe, they are greeted by a warm and inviting atmosphere that celebrates the natural beauty and charm of the island.


The Eluna Fairy Cafe boasts a charming and ethereal ambiance, with soft, twinkling fairy lights, delicate flower arrangements, and an abundance of vibrant greenery that creates an intimate and enchanting atmosphere. The interior is adorned with intricate woodwork, whimsical fairy decor, and cozy seating arrangements that allow guests to relax and immerse themselves in the fairy tale-like surroundings.


The menu at Eluna Fairy Cafe showcases a fusion of Avalonian flavors and fairy-inspired culinary creations. The skilled fairy chefs draw inspiration from the island's lush forests, pristine oceans, and mystical ingredients to craft a delectable array of dishes. Guests can expect a blend of fresh seafood, locally sourced vegetables, and herbs and spices imbued with a touch of magic, resulting in a culinary experience that is both delightful and memorable.


Fairy waitstaff, dressed in ethereal attire, provide attentive and personalized service, ensuring that each guest's dining experience is memorable and tailored to their preferences. The fairies add an extra touch of magic to the atmosphere, engaging guests with charming conversation and stories of Avalonia's folklore.


The dining experience at Eluna Fairy Cafe is a celebration of Avalonia's enchanting ambiance, culinary creativity, and impeccable service. With its fairy-inspired cuisine, whimsical atmosphere, and a commitment to creating memorable moments, the cafe provides a truly unforgettable dining journey for guests on the mystical island of Avalonia.
